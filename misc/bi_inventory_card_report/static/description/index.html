Specific Warehouse and Location Wise Stock Card Report

Inventory Stock Card Report Odoo app offers a detailed report that presents a thorough overview of stock movements, both incoming and outgoing, along with corresponding quantities. Users can easily generate these reports for specific time periods by selecting start and end dates. Additionally, they have the flexibility to create reports for individual products or entire product categories while filtering by warehouse or stock location. This app provides users with valuable insights into their inventory management, helping them make informed decisions about their stock levels and operations.

Features

Stock Card PDF & XLS Report

User can generate stock card PDF and XLS report.

Stock Card Report for Specific Period

User can generate stock card for specific period of selected dates.

Stock Card Report by Products or Product Category

User can generate stock card report by product or product category.

Stock Card Report by Specific Warehouse & Location

User can generate stock card report by warehouse and stock location.
